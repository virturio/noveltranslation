[Vol. 6] Chapter 97: No Escape Once You’re Here!

After leaving only the Light Elemental Spirit to protect the Peach Blossom Guardians just in case, Violet and the others continued deeper into the Peach Blossom Forest.   Soon, everything in front of them became clear.   They reached the peak platform above the sea of clouds after climbing the final gentle slope.   There were no more peach trees blocking their view.

The misty white clouds and clear fog swirled in the air, blending the place with the sky, making it feel like they were in a fairyland.   The platform, only about a hundred meters in diameter, was mostly occupied by a clear spiritual spring and pool in the center. Water from the spring flowed slowly down the mountain.
