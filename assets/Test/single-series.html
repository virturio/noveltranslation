Synopsis

Shen Yuntang was a beautiful shuanger, but he fell into the river with a lazy man from the village. His clothes were torn, and the lazy man looked at him, so he had no choice but to marry the lazy man as his husband. People in the village said that his life was over. The lazy man didn't even bother to get out of bed. He had almost sold all his family's fields and house, and Shen Yuntang would only suffer after marriage. Everyone guessed that Shen Yuntang must have been crying in secret, but instead, he got married happily. In his past life, Shen Yuntang had been the wife of a county magistrate...
